Introduction Physics has its roots stretched all around the universe. From smallest atom to mightiest universe, physics can explain everything. It has been developing since long back by Rishimunis of India, scholars of Greece and other remarkable scientists. The motto behind development of physics was to make the life more comfortable and more streamlined. Physics includes maths, electronics, mechanics, thermodynamics, kinetics, quantam phenomena, and many other fields. Physics is combination of more than one of its sub branches. To lighten the interdisciplinary aspect of physics and the motto behind development of physics we would like to present a device which senses the air and provides us information about the quality of air around us.
Motive With the rise in air pollution problems especially in areas like Punjab which are major patients of crop burning, it become necessary to have a check on the amount of the pollutants. So we need some device which inspects the air from surrounding, senses it and displays the exact amount of pollutants (in parts per million). If the pollutant level increases above the specified limit it becomes dangerous for health, so the device warns us with a buzzer in dangerous situations.
