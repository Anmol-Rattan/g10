The Archimedes Screw
Physics Tech Musuem
Desgined and Built by students of IIT Ropar

MThe Archimedes screw is an example of simple machine application that has survived the ages to fit diverse products in the modern era. Historians date the first evidence of Archimedes screw use around 250 B.C., and it is so-named because tradition suggests it was invented by the Syracusan natural philosopher and scientist Archimedes.
