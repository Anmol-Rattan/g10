Physics behind kalimba:- The length of steel springs will be different. They will be tuned according to the frequencies of different notes of music i.e. C D E F G A B C. So in total minimum 8 strings will be used. The length decides the frequency with which that string would be vibrating. For open end string, the formula for natural frequency becomes, Freq.=n*( Velocity of sound in air)/4*length , Which comes out to be inversely proportional to the length. So in this way length of the strings is decided.
